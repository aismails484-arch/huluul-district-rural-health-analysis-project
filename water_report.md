# The Nexus: Water Access and Regional Health

## 💧 Foundation of Health
Sustainable water access (established in Phase 1 of the Huluul Water Project) is the prerequisite for the **Huluul Regional Referral Hospital**. 

## 🏥 Clinical Integration
* **Dialysis Requirements:** The 15-station Hemodialysis unit requires a continuous supply of ultra-pure, treated water (RO System).
* **Sanitation & Hygiene:** 24/7 water availability is mandatory for the Surgical Theaters, NICU, and the General Wards to prevent healthcare-associated infections (HAIs).
* **Public Health:** Clean water access in the 4 MCH satellites will reduce water-borne diseases by an estimated 75%, allowing the hospital to focus on specialized tertiary care (Neurology, Orthopedics).
