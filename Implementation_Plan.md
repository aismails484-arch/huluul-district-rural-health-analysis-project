# Implementation Strategy: Huluul Regional Hospital

### Phase 1: Infrastructure & Diagnostics (Months 1-10)
* **Groundbreaking:** Construction of the main Diagnostic, Trauma, and Maternity wings.
* **Satellite Upgrades:** Equipping the 4 MCH sites (Ceel-Nimcoon, Laas-Tomare, Dhuur-Cilan, Ceel-Dibir).
* **Tech Deployment:** Installation of the 1.5T MRI and 15 Dialysis machines.

### Phase 2: Specialist Unit Launch (Months 11-18)
* **Staffing:** Recruitment of Neurosurgeons, Orthopedic Surgeons, and Dialysis Technicians.
* **Rehab Center:** Opening the Stroke Neuro-Physiotherapy Pavilion.

### Phase 3: Regional Integration (Months 19-24)
* **Full Capacity:** Launching the 250-bed wards and the Pediatric NICU.
* **Referral Network:** Establishing the 24/7 dispatch system for the ambulance fleet.
